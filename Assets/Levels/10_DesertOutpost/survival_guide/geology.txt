GEOLOGY.
Natural processes of Geology can be found all around the Georing. Scientists and researchers have even dated some rocks to be a few hundred million years old.
Very Gneiss!

From erosion to plate techtonics, sedimentation to volcanism... the Georing has it all! 

A varied selection of enviroments allows for a diverse and robust ecosystem of life to exist.
The Georing is a bit like a biological mosaic.
