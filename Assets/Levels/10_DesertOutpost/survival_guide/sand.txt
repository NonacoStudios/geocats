Sand is as abundant in the deserts as water is in the oceans.
The Great Litterbox consists of a unique kind of sand that begins to clump when wet.
In the past, it was mined extensively to supply metropolitan centres with a natural source of litter.

Use extreme caution when operating electronics in the desert.
Even a few grains of sand can cause circuitboards and memorycards to become corrupt.
Remember to back up your important data regularly.
